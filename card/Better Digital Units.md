# Better Digital Units

- b: bit
- p: pinch, 2 bits
- nb: nybble, 4 bits
- B: byte (8 bits)
- CH: chomp (2B) (precision)
- ET: entree(4B) (precision)
- FC: full-course(8B) (precision)
- nt: niti (nitee), 1024 (precision)
- pk: peka, 1024 dota
- mr: mora, 1024 peka
- ex: exta, 1024 mora
- tt: tetra, 1024 exta
- rv: rova, 1024 tetra
- jq: jelqa, 1024 rova
- wq: waqqu, 1024 jelqa
